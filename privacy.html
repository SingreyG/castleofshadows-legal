Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 May 2026

Castle of Shadows (the “Game”) is developed and published by Singreybuilds (“we”, “us”). This page explains what data the Game handles and how we treat it. We aim to keep things short and honest.

1. Summary

We do not collect, sell, or share any personally identifiable information. The Game stores your progress locally on your device. We do not display third-party advertising and we do not include third-party analytics in the version this policy covers.

2. Data the Game stores on your device

On first launch the Game generates a random anonymous identifier (e.g. anon_a1b2c3d4) and saves it together with your run progress, settings, and unlocked upgrades in your device’s local storage. This information never leaves your device unless you choose to contact us for support.

  • Random anonymous identifier
  • Game progress (souls, upgrades, statistics)
  • Preferences (sound, haptic, language, accessibility toggles)

You can erase all of this at any time from Settings → Reset progress inside the Game.

3. Data we do NOT collect

  • Your name, email, address, phone number, or any government ID
  • Your contact list, photos, microphone, or camera
  • Your precise or approximate location
  • Your device advertising identifier (IDFA / GAID)
  • Behavioural analytics events

4. Children

The Game is suitable for ages 9 and above. Because we do not collect personal data and do not show third-party ads, we do not knowingly collect data from children. The Game is not directed at children under 13 in the meaning of COPPA.

5. Third parties

Distribution platforms (Apple App Store, Google Play) collect their own diagnostic data subject to their privacy policies. We have no control over and no access to that data. The current version of the Game does not embed any other third-party SDKs.

6. If we add ads or analytics later

Future versions may introduce optional rewarded ads or anonymous gameplay analytics to support development. If that happens we will update this policy, bump the “Last updated” date, and present an in-game notice the first time the new behaviour is active.

7. Your rights

Under regulations such as the GDPR (EU), the UK GDPR, and the KVKK (Türkiye), you generally have the right to access, correct, delete, and export personal data we hold about you. As stated above, we hold no personal data on our own servers. If you contact us by email, the email address you write from is held only for the purpose of replying to you and is deleted after the conversation is closed.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or any related request, write to support@singreybuilds.com. We respond within a reasonable time, usually a few business days.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the Game evolves. The version on this page is always the current one; the “Last updated” date at the top tells you when it last changed.
